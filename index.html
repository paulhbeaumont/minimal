Working papers

1. Time Is Money: Cash-Flow Risk and Product Market Behavior
Last version / Technical appendix / Revised!

Long payment delays in the domestic market do represent a financial barrier to international trade. Estimations exploiting a 2009 French reform show that an exogenous decrease in payment delays of 10 days lowers the probability to exit an export market by 7.2% and raises the probability to enter a new one by 0.4%.
