Working papers

1. Time Is Money: Cash-Flow Risk and Product Market Behavior
Last version / New!

Long payment delays in the domestic market do represent a financial barrier to international trade. An exogenous 100% increase in payment delays in the French market raises the probability to exit an export market by 5.8%, lowers the probability of entry by -11.8% and increases the level of exports by 4.5% relative to their unconditional values.
